Bio

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins(born 5 May 1988) is ans English simger-songwriter. After graduating from the BRIT School for Performing Arts and Technology in 2006, Adele was given a recording contract by XL Recordings after a friend posted her demo on Myspace the same year.

Her debut album, 19, was released in 2008 to commercial and critical success. In 2011, 2012 and 2016, Billboard named Adele Artist of the Year. In 2012, she was listed at number five on VH1's 100 Greatest Women in Music. Time magazine named her one of the most influential people in the world in 2012 and 2016. With sales of more than 100 milion records, Adele is one of the best-selling music artists of all time.
